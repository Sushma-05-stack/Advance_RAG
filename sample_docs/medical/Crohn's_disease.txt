# Crohn's disease

## Overview
A chronic inflammatory disease of the intestines, especially the colon and ileum, associated with ulcers and fistulae.

## Diagnosis
Diagnosis of Crohn's disease typically involves clinical assessment, patient history, and specific laboratory tests or imaging. Early detection is crucial for effective management.

## Treatment
Treatment options vary depending on the severity but generally aim to manage symptoms and improve the patient's quality of life. Patients should consult healthcare providers for tailored treatment plans.
