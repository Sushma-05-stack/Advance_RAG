# Stroke

## Overview
A sudden disabling attack or loss of consciousness caused by an interruption in the flow of blood to the brain.

## Diagnosis
Diagnosis of Stroke typically involves clinical assessment, patient history, and specific laboratory tests or imaging. Early detection is crucial for effective management.

## Treatment
Treatment options vary depending on the severity but generally aim to manage symptoms and improve the patient's quality of life. Patients should consult healthcare providers for tailored treatment plans.
