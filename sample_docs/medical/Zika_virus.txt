# Zika virus

## Overview
A virus transmitted by mosquitoes which typically causes asymptomatic or mild infection (fever and rash) in humans.

## Diagnosis
Diagnosis of Zika virus typically involves clinical assessment, patient history, and specific laboratory tests or imaging. Early detection is crucial for effective management.

## Treatment
Treatment options vary depending on the severity but generally aim to manage symptoms and improve the patient's quality of life. Patients should consult healthcare providers for tailored treatment plans.
