# Alzheimer's

## Overview
Progressive mental deterioration that can occur in middle or old age, due to generalized degeneration of the brain.

## Diagnosis
Diagnosis of Alzheimer's typically involves clinical assessment, patient history, and specific laboratory tests or imaging. Early detection is crucial for effective management.

## Treatment
Treatment options vary depending on the severity but generally aim to manage symptoms and improve the patient's quality of life. Patients should consult healthcare providers for tailored treatment plans.
