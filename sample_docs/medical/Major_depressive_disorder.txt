# Major depressive disorder

## Overview
A mental disorder characterized by at least two weeks of pervasive low mood, low self-esteem, and loss of interest.

## Diagnosis
Diagnosis of Major depressive disorder typically involves clinical assessment, patient history, and specific laboratory tests or imaging. Early detection is crucial for effective management.

## Treatment
Treatment options vary depending on the severity but generally aim to manage symptoms and improve the patient's quality of life. Patients should consult healthcare providers for tailored treatment plans.
