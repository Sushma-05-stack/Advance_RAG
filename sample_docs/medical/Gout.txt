# Gout

## Overview
A disease in which defective metabolism of uric acid causes arthritis, especially in the smaller bones of the feet.

## Diagnosis
Diagnosis of Gout typically involves clinical assessment, patient history, and specific laboratory tests or imaging. Early detection is crucial for effective management.

## Treatment
Treatment options vary depending on the severity but generally aim to manage symptoms and improve the patient's quality of life. Patients should consult healthcare providers for tailored treatment plans.
