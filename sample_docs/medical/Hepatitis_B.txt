# Hepatitis B

## Overview
A severe form of viral hepatitis transmitted in infected blood, causing fever, debility, and jaundice.

## Diagnosis
Diagnosis of Hepatitis B typically involves clinical assessment, patient history, and specific laboratory tests or imaging. Early detection is crucial for effective management.

## Treatment
Treatment options vary depending on the severity but generally aim to manage symptoms and improve the patient's quality of life. Patients should consult healthcare providers for tailored treatment plans.
