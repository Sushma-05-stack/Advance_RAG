# COPD

## Overview
Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, a type of obstructive lung disease characterized by long-term breathing problems and poor airflow.

## Diagnosis
Diagnosis of COPD typically involves clinical assessment, patient history, and specific laboratory tests or imaging. Early detection is crucial for effective management.

## Treatment
Treatment options vary depending on the severity but generally aim to manage symptoms and improve the patient's quality of life. Patients should consult healthcare providers for tailored treatment plans.
