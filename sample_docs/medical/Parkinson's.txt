# Parkinson's

## Overview
A progressive disease of the nervous system marked by tremor, muscular rigidity, and slow, imprecise movement.

## Diagnosis
Diagnosis of Parkinson's typically involves clinical assessment, patient history, and specific laboratory tests or imaging. Early detection is crucial for effective management.

## Treatment
Treatment options vary depending on the severity but generally aim to manage symptoms and improve the patient's quality of life. Patients should consult healthcare providers for tailored treatment plans.
