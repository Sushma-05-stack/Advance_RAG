COMPANY POLICY HANDBOOK
========================

1. WORK HOURS
Full-time employees work 40 hours per week, Monday through Friday.
Core hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Flexible start times between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM.

2. LEAVE POLICY
Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid annual leave per year.
Sick leave: 10 days per year.
Parental leave: 12 weeks paid for primary caregivers.

3. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
Expenses under $50: No pre-approval required.
Expenses $50-$500: Manager approval required.
Expenses over $500: Director approval required.
Submit receipts within 30 days.

4. REMOTE WORK
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week.
Fully remote positions require VP approval.

5. RESIGNATION
Standard notice period is 2 weeks.
Senior roles (Director+): 4 weeks notice required.

6. LEARNING BUDGET
Each employee receives $1,500 per year for professional development.
